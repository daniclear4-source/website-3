Our Services

Holistic Therapies for Complete Wellness

Each therapy is designed to restore your natural balance and promote healing from within, using time-tested methods and natural ingredients.

Panchgavya Therapy

Harness the divine healing power of five sacred cow products — milk, curd, ghee, urine, and dung — for deep body purification and immune strengthening.

Panchakarma & Marma Therapy

Experience the ancient five-step detoxification process combined with vital energy point stimulation for complete body rejuvenation and toxin removal.

Naturopathy

Tap into the body's innate healing ability through natural therapies including hydrotherapy, mud therapy, and lifestyle modifications for sustainable wellness.

Acupuncture & Acupressure

Restore the flow of life energy through precise pressure point stimulation, relieving chronic pain, stress, and promoting natural healing responses.

Dietetics & Nutrition

Receive personalized Ayurvedic dietary plans tailored to your body constitution (Prakriti) for optimal health, digestion, and natural weight management.

Yoga & Meditation

Discover inner balance through guided yoga sessions and meditation practices designed to calm the mind, strengthen the body, and elevate spiritual awareness.
